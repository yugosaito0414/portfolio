History of Raleigh Waffle and How I Started from the Scratch

At the age of 24, I talked to my one of my best friend, Takumi and decided to do the business with him. Takumi helped me to build a food truck and made a menu together. I was very lucky to have him as a friend. Throughout our foodtruck business, I learned so many things about life and how business works in actual life. I hope this experice will make me a better person. At the end, this business might not work, however, the time I spent with Takumi was wonderful and will stay as one of the best memories.
